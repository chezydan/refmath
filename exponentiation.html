Exponentiation

exponentiation - rules

Multiplication Rule

\begin{equation} a^x a^y = a^{x+y} \end{equation}

Division Rule

\begin{equation} a^x / a^y = a^{x-y} \end{equation}

Power of a Power Rule

\begin{equation} (a^x)^y = a^{xy} \end{equation}

Power of a Fraction Rule

\begin{equation} (\frac{a}{b})^x = \frac{a^x}{b^x} \end{equation}

0 exponent Rule

\begin{equation} a^0 = 1 \end{equation}

Negative Rule

\begin{equation} a^{-x} = \frac{1}{a^x} \end{equation}

Fractional Exponent Rule

\begin{equation} a^{\frac{x}{y}} = \sqrt[y]{a^x} \end{equation}
