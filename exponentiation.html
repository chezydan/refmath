Exponentation

exponentiation - rules

product

\begin{equation} a^x a^y = a^{x+y} \end{equation}

quotient

\begin{equation} \frac{a^x}{ a^y} = a^{x-y} \end{equation}

power

\begin{equation} (x^a)^b = x^{ab} \end{equation}

power

\begin{equation} (ab)^x = a^x b^x \end{equation}

power of negative 1

\begin{equation} x^{-1} = \frac{1}{x} \end{equation}

change of signs

\begin{equation} a^{-x} = \frac{1} {a^x} \end{equation}

fractional

\begin{equation} a^\frac{x}{y} = \sqrt[y]{a^x} = (\sqrt[y]{a})^x\end{equation}
