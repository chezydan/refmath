Logarithms

Logarithms - rules

1 rule

\begin{equation} log_b(1)=0 \end{equation}

identity rule
\begin{equation} log_b(b)=1 \end{equation}
log of exp rule
\begin{equation} log_b(b^k)=k \end{equation}
Product rule

\begin{equation} log_a(xy) = log_a(x) + log_a(y) \end{equation}

Quotient rule

\begin{equation} log_a(\frac{x}{y}) = log_a(x) - log_a(y) \end{equation}

Power rule

\begin{equation} r \. log_a(x) = log_a(x^r) \end{equation}

Change of Base rule

\begin{equation} log_a(x) = \frac{log_b(x)}{log_b(a)} \end{equation}
